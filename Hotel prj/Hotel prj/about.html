Our History

Founded in 1926 by the Aura family, Aura Grande has hosted dignitaries, celebrities, and families from all over the world. Our architecture blends classic heritage with modern luxury.

We believe in delivering more than just a room; we provide an atmosphere of quiet luxury and impeccable care.
